SheHacks 2K19

Dream ● Discover ● Develop

SheHacks is an all women hackathon taking place at GEC Palakkad under the banner of IEDC and the patronage of Kerala Startup Mission. Supported by the institution's Women Cell, the event focuses on providing a platform for innovative and creative minds among women techies to showcase their pioneering ideas. In addition to the hackathon, the program will be adorned with talks by eminent speakers and entrepreneurs. Vibing with an aura of mystifying grandeur, and fortifying the amalgamation of technology with brilliant ideas, SheHacks is a technical extravaganza cultivating bright minds with even brighter imagination. With an array of enthralling festivities, the event seeks to create an unforgettable experience for the participants. So don't wait by, the clock is ticking, hurry up and pave your way to the world of opportunities through SheHacks 2K19!

Kerala Startup Mission



Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM, formerly known as Technopark TBI) is the central agency of the Government of Kerala for entrepreneurship development and incubation activities in Kerala, India. KSUM was primarily founded to undertake the planning, establishment, and management of the Technology Business Incubator (TBI), a startup accelerator in Kerala, to promote technology-based entrepreneurship activities, and to create the infrastructure and environment required to support high-technology-based businesses.

IEDC GEC Palakkad

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) is being promoted in educational institutions to develop institutional mechanism to create entrepreneurial culture in S&T academic institutions and to foster techno-entrepreneurship for generation of wealth and employment by S&T persons. The IEDCs are established to promote innovation and entrepreneurship among the students and thus equipping them to be job providers rather than job seekers as they pass out of the institution..

SHE Women Cell GEC Palakkad

The women cell of GEC Palakkad comprises of the women enthusiasts of GEC consisiting of both students and the faculty. The cell has been actively working on solving various women related issues and encouraging girls to come closer to the various technological and entrepreneurial activities. She in Tech, SheRun 2019, etc have been few of the many accomplishments of the cell. The cell has so far been successful in its primary focus on enhancing the knowledge and skills of the female students of the college.
